While on a road trip, there are several routes that you can take to experience the breathtaking scenery of nature.

Do you want to view Rizal in its entirety? Take Ortigas Avenue until Taytay Diversion Road is reached. Continue on this road until you reach Manila East Road. Other Rizal municipalities you'll pass through are Cainta, Antipolo, Teresa, Morong, Baras, Tanay, and Pililia. You can visit Taytay, Angono, Binangonan, and Cardona by taking a different route.
