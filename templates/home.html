Enjoy wonderful experiences in Rizal Province

Rizal Province is a place where you can reconnect with nature while still enjoying the finest aspects of city life. From the steep mountains of Montalban and Antipolo down to the gorgeous coastline of Laguna de Bay, lush hills and undulating valleys stretch. In the middle of rolling hills, lakeshores, caverns, and waterfalls, the terrain offers fascinating chances for biking, mountain climbing, and camping.

Cycling

Very accesible from Metro Manila with many uphill battles with rocky roads, but it is time well spent for the view, especially if you ride early in the morning when there are less people. Mountain bikes are the ideal choice for terrain conditions in order to avoid injury.

Hiking

The mountains of Rizal are one of the reasons for its fame. Some are beginner-friendly, making them ideal day getaways from Metro Manila or neighboring provinces and cities. It's also a convenient venue to get to, whether you're via private or public transit.
